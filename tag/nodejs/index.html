nodejs

ExpertaLabs

Under the [ExpertaLabs](https://expertalabs.com/) brand, we automated dozens of test cases for [Flowmon Network a.s.](https://www.flowmon.com/). Both pure back-end test cases in Python and Pytest, and GUI test cases in Cypress.

Mapotic

For Mapotic s.r.o. we created the foundations of a unified platform for quick and simple creation of individual client applications. The platform allows the creation of a new application for live motion tracking just by configuration without the involvement of any developer.

BrnoUrbanGrid and DataMapo

Under the banner of the company [Altimapo](http://altimapo.cz/) we run grid statistics for professionals and the audience. The BrnoUrbanGrid application covers the city of Brno and the Brno metropolitan area, while the DataMapo application covers the entire Czech Republic.

Geoportals

Together with [RILOG](https://www.vukoz.cz/) and [CzechGlobe](https://www.czechglobe.cz/en/) we present geospatial data to both general and professional public in the form of interactive maps.

Scientific research projects

We have long-term cooperation with Masaryk University, especially with the [Institute of Geography](https://geogr.sci.muni.cz/en) of the Faculty of Science on scientific research projects, for example Modeling the daily dynamics of the number of people present in an urban space (MODUS) or Stress of Citizens and Entropy in Urban Setting (STRECITY).

Consultancy

We offer consulting in the field of complex web applications, geospatial data processing and interactive maps. We regularly help e.g. [ICS MU](https://www.ics.muni.cz/en) with the system Kompas and the library munimap, and the [Institute of Geonics](https://geonika.cz/index.html) of the Czech Academy of Sciences.
